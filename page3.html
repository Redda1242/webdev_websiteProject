Characters

Character colors

The user gets to customize there character. They can change the color of their character as much as they want before the start of the session. However, there can not be repeated colors, so there can't be two white or two black crewmates.

The available colors are:

  1. Red
  2. Blue
  3. White
  4. Black
  5. Light green
  6. Dark green
  7. Aqua blue
  8. Dark blue
  9. Yellow
  10. Brown
  11. Orange
  12. Purple

Pets

Users can also get pets that will follow them around, however these are not free and to get them you need to pay for them. As pet, you could get different animals or little crewmates.

Costumes

The user can choose different types of dresses to dress their character. They caan also use different hats to make their character represent them and to have fun.
