What is among us??

Among us is an online game created by Innersloth on June 15, 2018. This game is based on space where a maximum of 10 users can join. One of the crewmates is an imposter and the crewmates need to complete tasks and/or guess who the imposter is and vote him out before everyone gets killed.

The rules of the game..

If you are a crewmate: If you are an imposter:
  1. Complete all tasks
  2. Report dead bodies
  3. Do report suspicious players
  4. Vote out Imposter
  5. Do not cheat!
  1. Kill crewmates without looking suspicious
  2. Pretend to be a crewmate and do not expose yourself
  3. Fake tasks
  4. Try to look innocent and blame others to survive
  5. You will win once the number of imposters and number of crewmates are the same

Popularity?

Among us was more popular in South Korea and Brazil until 2020 where it became famous in the US and the world in general, Which started when a twitch streamer Sodapoppin first streamed himself playing the game.Many other influencers did the same after him and the game became much more famous.
